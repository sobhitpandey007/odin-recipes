Penne Arrabbiata Pasta

Description

Penne Arrabbiata is a classic Italian pasta dish made with penne pasta tossed in a spicy tomato sauce flavored with garlic, olive oil, and red chili flakes.

This simple yet flavorful recipe is quick to prepare and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 250g penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the penne pasta.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan.
  3. Cook the garlic and chili flakes.
  4. Add the tomato puree and seasonings.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked pasta and mix well.
  7. Serve hot.
