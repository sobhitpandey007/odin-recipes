lasagna

description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and flavorful seasonings. It is baked until hot and golden brown.

This easy homemade lasagna is perfect for family dinners, special occasions, or meal prep. It is cheesy, filling, and packed with delicious flavors.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning

steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Cook the onion, garlic, and ground beef until browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and seasonings, then simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Mix the ricotta cheese with the egg.
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  8. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake for 35–40 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  10. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
